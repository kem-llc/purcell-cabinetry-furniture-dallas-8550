Our story

Our Story: Crafting Excellence in Dallas

To create beautiful, functional woodwork that enriches homes and stands the test of time.

Purcell Cabinetry & Furniture was founded over two decades ago with a simple mission: bring heirloom-quality craftsmanship to every home in Dallas. What started as a one-man workshop has grown into a trusted name for custom cabinetry, furniture, and millwork. Our founder, a master carpenter with a passion for wood, built the business on principles of integrity, precision, and personalized service.

Today, we continue that tradition. Every project — whether a full kitchen remodel or a single piece of furniture — receives the same dedication to quality. We source the finest materials, employ time-honored techniques, and work closely with each client to ensure the final result exceeds expectations. We're proud to be part of the Dallas community and to see our work in homes across the city.
